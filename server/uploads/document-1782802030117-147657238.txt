# Turbine Generator A - Maintenance Manual

## 1. Routine Inspection
- Check bearing vibration levels daily. Normal levels should not exceed 10mm/s.
- Inspect hydraulic fluid temperature. Normal operating range is 45°C - 65°C.

## 2. Emergency Shutdown Procedure
If vibration exceeds 15mm/s or temperature spikes above 85°C:
1. Initiate emergency cut-off.
2. Evacuate immediate vicinity.
3. Contact the chief engineer.

## 3. Maintenance Schedule
- Oil change every 500 operating hours.
- Filter replacement every month.
