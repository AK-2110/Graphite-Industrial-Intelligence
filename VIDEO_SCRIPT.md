# 3-4 Minute Demo Video Script & Outline

*Use this script as a guide when recording your screen using a tool like OBS Studio, Loom, or Zoom.*

### 0:00 - 0:30 | Introduction & The Problem
**Visual:** Start on the **Home Dashboard** (showcasing the animated hero section).
**Script:** 
> "Hi, I'm presenting **Graphite Industrial Intelligence**. In heavy industry, when a critical machine goes offline, operators waste hours cross-referencing sensor alerts with massive, 1,000-page technical manuals just to find the fix. Graphite solves this by unifying live hardware telemetry with AI-powered document search."

### 0:30 - 1:00 | Asset Registry
**Visual:** Click on **View Asset Registry**. Scroll through the list of equipment. Click on an offline/maintenance asset to open the detailed modal.
**Script:** 
> "It starts with the Asset Registry, the digital twin of our factory floor. Here, we track the live health status of every piece of equipment—from turbines to hydraulic presses. As you can see, we have an asset currently marked as 'Offline', requiring immediate attention."

### 1:00 - 1:45 | Document Ingestion
**Visual:** Navigate to **Document Ingestion**. Show the upload area and the list of already indexed manuals.
**Script:** 
> "To understand *how* to fix that asset, we ingest OEM technical manuals. In this module, we upload massive PDFs or text files. The Graphite backend instantly parses, chunks, and vectorizes these documents using Google's Gemini AI, turning static text into a searchable semantic database."

### 1:45 - 2:45 | The Knowledge Graph (The 'Wow' Factor)
**Visual:** Navigate to the **Knowledge Graph**. Zoom in and out. Drag the nodes around to show interactivity. Click on an Anomaly node, then click "Analyze Root Cause".
**Script:** 
> "This is where the magic happens: The Knowledge Graph. It visually maps the relationships between our physical assets and the ingested documentation. When an asset fails, an anomaly node appears. Instead of hunting through manuals, an operator simply clicks the anomaly and runs an AI-powered Root Cause Analysis. The system queries our semantic database and instantly streams back the exact repair procedure directly on the graph."

### 2:45 - 3:30 | AI Knowledge Q&A
**Visual:** Navigate to **Knowledge Q&A**. Type a question like: *"What is the standard operating temperature for a turbine, and what should I do if it overheats?"* Show the AI generating the response.
**Script:** 
> "For more specific troubleshooting, operators can use the AI Knowledge Q&A. This is a full Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline. You can ask complex maintenance questions, and the AI will scan all uploaded manuals to give you a precise, cited answer in seconds."

### 3:30 - 4:00 | Conclusion
**Visual:** Return to the **Home Dashboard**, scrolling slowly through the Architecture timeline.
**Script:** 
> "Graphite Industrial Intelligence takes the guesswork out of industrial maintenance, reducing downtime and saving millions in operational costs. Thank you for watching."
